    When was the revolt of the Northern Earls?
    1569
    Which Nobles lead the Revolt of the Northern Earls?
    Earl of Northumberland, Earl of Westmorland
    What were the aims of the Revolt of the Northern Earls?
    - Replace Elizabeth with Mary
    - Make England Catholic instead of Protestant
    What were the key events of the Revolt of the Northern Earls?
    9th - 15th of November:
    Earls of Northumberland and Westmorland raised armies from their tenants
    Marched on Durham

    22nd - 30th of November:
    Rebels marched South

    16th-19th of December:
    Elizabeth's Royal Army marched and caused the rebels to flee.
    Earl of Northumberland beheaded and Northumberland handed over to Elizabeth.

    Why was being excommunicated a problem for Elizabeth?
